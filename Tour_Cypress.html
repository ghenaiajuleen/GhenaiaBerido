Japan

I have always wanted to witness the cherry blossoms in full bloom, which is why I want to travel to Japan. Walking beneath those stunning pink and white trees seems like a heavenly experience. Japan's winters, with their serene snowy scenery and warm customs like winter festivals and hot springs, are another thing I'd like to experience. It looks like the perfect fusion of culture and nature that I've always desired.
