France (Paris)

I want to visit Paris because I really want to see the Eiffel Tower or as we all know the “City of Love”. It’s one of the most famous landmarks in the world. The tower's romantic ambiance, especially at sunset or with its twinkling lights at night, makes it a popular spot for seeking a magical experience. Being in Paris means I get to explore the charming streets, enjoy the food, and experience the city’s unique culture. It's one of those once-in-a-lifetime experiences I don't want to miss.
