Canada

I've always wanted to visit Canada, and one of the biggest reasons is to experience the Aurora Borealis in person. The thought of seeing those colorful, changing lights move through the northern skies has a certain attraction. I've seen a lot of pictures, but I can only image how amazing it must be to see them in person. They move and change color like if the sky is alive, and I would really like to see that for myself.
